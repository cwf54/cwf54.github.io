Introduction

In this lab, we are exploring the uses of an Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU). The sensor we are using is a board from Sparkfun that conveniently connected to the QWIIC breakout board the TOF sensors were connected to in Lab 3.

The Sparkfun IMU board

This IMU contains a "3-axis gyroscope, 3-axis accelerometer, 3-axis compass, and a Digital Motion Processor™" as per the data sheet. For this lab, we will be focusing on the data collected by the accelerometer and the gyroscope.
